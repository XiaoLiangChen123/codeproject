About Me

Hello! My name is Xiao Chen, and I am an art student passionate about exploring creativity through various mediums. My work reflects my journey, emotions, and inspirations. I strive to create pieces that resonate with others and tell a story.

My School

I am currently studying at Brooklyn College, where I am honing my skills and learning from talented professors and peers. The vibrant art community at Brooklyn College has been instrumental in shaping my artistic vision and pushing me to grow as an artist.

My Process

My creative process begins with observation and inspiration. I often sketch out ideas and experiment with different techniques before committing to a final piece. Whether it's painting, drawing, or digital art, I enjoy exploring new methods to bring my ideas to life.
